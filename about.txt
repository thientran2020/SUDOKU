Author: Thien Tran

Description: GUI Sudoku Game
5 buttons:
NEW: Randomly pick a new sudoku
CHECK: Check whether the player's solution is correct and Display relevant message
REMOVE: Remove incorrect filled numbers
CLEAR: Clear all - Restore the initial status of the current Sudoku
SOLVE: Display solution

Dataset: http://www.mathsphere.co.uk/resources/MathSphereFreeResourcesSudoku.html
